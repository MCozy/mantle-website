Support & FAQ

Everything you need to get the most out of Mantle.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Sign up with Apple, Google, or email and password. You can create an account directly from the app when you first open it.
Is the app free?
Yes, Mantle is completely free. There are no subscriptions, in-app purchases, or hidden fees.
Is Mantle available on Android?
Not yet. Mantle is currently available on iOS only. Android support is planned for a future release.
How long does setup take?
Under 5 minutes. Enter your address, select your home systems, and your personalized maintenance schedule is generated instantly.

Your Schedule

How is my maintenance schedule created?
Our AI analyzes your home's age, location, climate, and installed systems to generate a personalized set of maintenance tasks with appropriate frequencies and seasonal timing.
Can I add my own tasks?
Yes. You can add custom tasks with all the same fields as AI-generated ones, including descriptions, instructions, supplies, cost estimates, and recurrence schedules.
What happens when I complete a task?
The task is marked as done and automatically advances to its next due date based on its recurrence schedule.
Can I skip or reschedule a task?
Yes. Skipping a task defers it to the next scheduled occurrence. Rescheduling lets you pick a specific new date.
Can I disable a task I don't need?
Yes. Disabled tasks are hidden from your active schedule and won't generate notifications. You can re-enable them at any time.

Home & Systems

Can I edit my home systems after setup?
Yes. Go to My Home to add or remove systems at any time. Your maintenance tasks will automatically update to reflect the changes.
What if I change the year my home was built?
Your AI-generated tasks will be regenerated based on the updated home age to ensure recommendations are accurate for your property.
Can I manage multiple homes?
Yes. Each home has its own separate schedule, tasks, household members, and professional contacts. Switch between homes from the home selector.

Household Sharing

How do I invite someone to my home?
Go to My Home, then Household, then tap Invite. You can send an invitation by email to anyone you'd like to join your home.
What can household members do?
Members can view all tasks, mark them as complete, skip or reschedule tasks, and add professional contacts. The home owner retains full control over membership and settings.
Can I remove a household member?
Yes. Home owners can remove any member from the household at any time via My Home.

Professional Contacts

How do I add a contractor?
Go to the Pros tab and tap the add button. You can search for local businesses or add a contractor manually with their name, phone number, and specialty.
Can I assign a pro to a task?
Yes. From any task detail screen, you can link up to 5 professional contacts. This makes it easy to remember who to call for each type of maintenance.

Account & Privacy

Is my data private?
Yes. We do not sell or share your personal data. Your information is only used to provide and improve the Mantle service. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I delete my account?
Contact us at trymantleapp@gmail.com and we will delete your account and all associated data.
How do I sign out?
Go to My Home, then Settings, then tap Sign out.
